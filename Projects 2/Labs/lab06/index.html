Website Evaluation

Website Information

URL: https://www.espn.com

Name: ESPN

Target Audience: The target audience are people who watch sports like football, basketball and baseball. It’s mainly for sports fans who want to get updates and news on these sports or thier teams.

Site Organization

The site has a top menu with sections like scores, sports and news, Live scores are shown at the top for quick updates.The homepage has a big image of the latest news, headlines, and links to articles.

CRAP Design Principle Used

Contrast: The site uses white text on dark backgrounds to make things stand out. Headlines are bold, and different colors are used to separate sections.

Accessibility Audit Score

I ran an accessibility check using the Google Lighthouse tool, and ESPN got a score of **85/100**. Some text has low contrast, and a few images don’t have alt text.

Effectiveness

The website does a great job of giving quick sports updates. It’s easy to find scores, news, and video highlights without clicking too much.The website does good job of providing straight information on sports. Finding scores, headlines, and video highlights is easy and takes little clicks.

Efficiency

Instant access to live scores is available to users. The search bar allows  quick finding of teams and articles. For real-time sports updates, the site loads quickly..

Engagement

With videos, live updates, and social media links, the website is very active.It has a lot of pictures and is eye-catching.

Recommendations for Improvement

All photos should have alt text added so that screen readers can explain them.To make the writing easier to read, fix contrast problems.To make the website more touch-friendly, increase the button sizes and spacing.For improved navigation, make sure links have readable, detailed descriptions.
