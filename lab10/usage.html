How is WoT Discovery Used?

In order to employ WoT Discovery, devices deliver Thing Descriptions (TDs) through a variety of channels, including direct advertising and directory service registration. These announcements can be subscribed to or searched by other devices or applications to locate devices within the network.

For example, a smart thermostat can announce itself on the network using multicast. A mobile app can detect it and automatically present controls to the user, based on the TD provided.

Multicast, for instance, can be used by a smart thermostat to broadcast itself throughout the network. Based on the given TD, a mobile app can identify it and provide the user the controls automatically.

Developers use industry-standard protocols like HTTP, CoAP, and MQTT to accomplish WoT Discovery. For machine readability, the Thing Descriptions are usually saved in JSON-LD format.
